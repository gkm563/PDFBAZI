Privacy & Legal Policy

Last Updated: January 11, 2026

1. Zero-Server Architecture

PDFBAZI is built on the principle of Absolute Privacy. Unlike traditional PDF editors, we do not use cloud servers to process your documents.

Your files never leave your computer. 100% of the processing happens in your browser.

We leverage advanced WebAssembly (WASM) technology to run high-performance C++ and Rust logic directly on your local machine. This ensures that your sensitive data remains under your control at all times.

2. No Data Collection

We believe that "No Data is the Safest Data." PDFBAZI does not collect, store, or sell any personal information.

  • No file logging or temporary storage.
  • No tracking cookies or invasive analytics.
  • No account registration required for core tools.

3. Terms of Use

PDFBAZI is provided as a free utility. While we ensure the highest standards of security through our local-processing architecture, the user is responsible for the content of the files they process and must comply with their local laws regarding document handling.
